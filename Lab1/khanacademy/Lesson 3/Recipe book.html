Recipe #1: Macarons

  • Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: 12 macarons
For Macarons:
Ingredients Quantity
Almond flour 100 g
Powdered sugar 150 g
Egg whites 90 g
Granulated sugar 100 g
Food coloring Optional, a few drops
For the filling:
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 100 g
Powdered sugar 150 g
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Raspberry jam (or any flavor) 2 tbsp

Step 1: Sift together almond flour and powdered sugar. Set aside.

Step 2: Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form, then gradually add granulated sugar and continue beating until glossy.

Step 3: Gently fold the dry ingredients into the meringue, add food coloring if desired, and pipe onto parchment paper in small circles.

Step 4: Let the macarons rest for 30 minutes, then bake at 150°C (300°F) for 15-18 minutes.

Step 5: Let the macarons cool completely before filling with buttercream or jam.
